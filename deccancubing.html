HOME

Hello! Welcome to Deccan Cubing's Page! You might be wondering what exactly is Deccan Cubing? Deccan Cubing is a community which strives in devoloping speedcubing in Hyderabad and South India. All details of official and unofficial competitions organized by our team would be shared here! With the support of our team members and all the awesome cubers across India, we hope that we will make speedcubing more popular and competitive!

WHAT IS SPEEDCUBING?

Previous Next

Speedcubing (also known as speedsolving) is a sport involving solving a variety of combination or twisty puzzles, the most famous being the 3x3x3 puzzle or Rubik's Cube, as quickly as possible. The current world record for solving the 3x3 puzzle is 3.47 seconds!

ABOUT US

Deccan Cubing was found in 2017 by the delegate of Hyderabad, Akula Pavan Kumar along with his friend Murali Krishna! The first official cubing competition by Deccan Cubing team was organized in August 2017! Since then Deccan Cubing has organized 12 official WCA Competitions!

CO-FOUNDERS

AKULA PAVAN KUMAR

Pavan is one of the co-founders of Deccan Cubing Organization and also is one of the seven delegates of India! He has been in this cubing community for over 10 years now and always makes sure that all rules are abided during the competition. He guides the other team members to make sure there is no confusion and the competition is organized smoothly!

MURALI KRISHNA

Murali is the 2nd co-founder of this organization. He has been attending official competitions since 2012! He usually is the one who finalizes the venues for our competitions and clears queries of participants and parents at the competition. He also takes care of the registrations which are done before the competition!

AKULA SAI KUMAR

Akula Sai Kumar, the younger brother of Pavan is equally important to the team as he is the one who takes care of all the scorecards, venue setups, banners and many more things! He also manages the registration along with Murali on the day of the competition and is really active in whatever he does!

Abhishek is the scrambling head of the head! He makes sure that every competitior's cube is scrambled in the correct way and there are no errors! He also plays a major role in advising the co-founders and volunteering members whenever required!
