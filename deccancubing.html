HOME




Hello! Welcome to Deccan Cubing's Page! You might be wondering what exactly is Deccan Cubing? Deccan Cubing is a community which strives in devoloping speedcubing in Hyderabad and South India. All details of official and unofficial competitions organized by our team would be shared here! With the support of our team members and all the awesome cubers across India, we hope that we will make speedcubing more popular and competitive!

WHAT IS SPEEDCUBING?

Previous Next

Speedcubing (also known as speedsolving) is a sport involving solving a variety of combination or twisty puzzles, the most famous being the 3x3x3 puzzle or Rubik's Cube, as quickly as possible. The current world record for solving the 3x3 puzzle is 3.47 seconds!

Deccan Cubing was found in 2017 by the delegate of Hyderabad, Akula Pavan Kumar along with his friend Murali Krishna! The first official cubing competition by Deccan Cubing team was organized in August 2017! Since then Deccan Cubing has organized 12 official WCA Competitions!
